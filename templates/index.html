Welcome to Wine-Disco!

Wine-Disco is not a nightclub, but rather a discovery tool for the Hungarian wine culture. Later other countries might be added to the tool.

Using Wine-Disco lets you explore the different regions and grapes of Hungary. More specifically you can browse the following entities:

  • Wine regions
  • Wine subregions
  • Grapes
  • Wineries

If you just open up the tool, you will see a graph where each node represents either a wine region or a wine subregion.

Double-click on a node and it will expand to show what other entities it relates to.

Right-click on one and you will see more details about that node. In the details you can also find links, pointing to other nodes. Feel free to click on those. In certain cases you can even find links outgoing from this page, for example taking you to site of a winery

Enjoy the discovery and drink good wine (with moderation of course)!
